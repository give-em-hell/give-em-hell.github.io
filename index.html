Give 'em Hell - a personal blog

"Give 'em hell, give 'em hell, stand up and yell!" is one particular line in Auburn University's fight song, "War Eagle." To me, "give 'em hell" is a rallying cry to stand out, to have some spine, some personality. To that end, this blog is a memorial to the way in which I see the little bits and pieces of life around me, the minutiae that built me up and broke me down.
