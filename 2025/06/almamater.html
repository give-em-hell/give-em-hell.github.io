Hail Alma Mater

Hail Alma Mater

Where you with your divine providence and outstretched arms

and full of that grand spirit of neutrality and liberty

from the Cuyahoga Side to the Mekong

put something firm enthroned in every heart.

Hail Alma Mater

Where you stood on cloud nine and went back to a normal life

ignorant that you are and still are and still will be deplorable

stood over nineteen nineteen twenty twenty nineteen/p>

and decided that justice had prevailed.

Hail Alma Mater

Where you saw the coming generations thronging to seek her side

where on that holy day of dynasty-felling you came and sought exceptionalism

and presided over the throne flanked by peace and fury

and dedicated but thirteen seconds to their pleas.

Hail Alma Mater

Where we rallied round the vaunted halls and the columns of the Merrill

and we dared to struggle and dared to win and killed our parents and smoked in

and napalmed the dog and carried its burning body round

during break as the flames went forward.

Hail Alma Mater

Where we may blacken the skies once more with stones and invite tragedy

and we bums all of us will look through all that is clumsy and condescending

and we will gather around you Alma Mater mother of heroes

and Hail to Thee Alma Mater.
